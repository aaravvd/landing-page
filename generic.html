My Experience

Internships, research projects, and more :)

Intern Experience

I've interned at a startup, called WellPlanned Inc, which creats AI-generated travel plans for users. In this data science internship, I created a machine learnimg model using a random forest regressor to predict the cost of user's travels. I deployed this model as a FastAPI, and had the website connect to one of the endpoints.

As a researcher, I've researched in a research group with the mentorship of PhD student in applied mathematics Christopher Degrendele(UCSC), on the topic of aerodynamic shape optimization, as well as solving partial differential equations using Physics Informed Neural Networks, in comparison to typical Computational Fluid Dynamics. I've trained a Phyics-Informed Neural Network to predict PDE solutions with up to 98% accuracy, and the PINN was over 3 times faster than traditional CFD methods.
